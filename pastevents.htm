American Cancer Society Relay for Life 2007

 

 

Picture

Mississippi Bangla Academy (MBA) proudly participated in the “Relay for life”, a fund raising event, sponsored by American Cancer Society (ACS).  Children and adults from local Bangali community enthusiastically joined this relay. This endeavor was to show our support to those whose lives have been touched by cancer.  The relay was held at Millsaps College football stadium on Friday, May the 4th, 2007, and was featured in the local newspaper.   MBA established a food stall and organized a children's cultural program.  The  Campsite Food Championship award went to MBA. Fund generated at this event was donated to ACS.

Picture

Pohela Baishakh, the first day of Bnagla New Year,  was celebrated with a cultural program by Mississippi Bangali Community  on April 14, 2007,  at Tougaloo College Holmes Auditorium, Jackson, Mississippi.  The program included recitations, dances, instrumental, and  solo, duet and chorus songs.
