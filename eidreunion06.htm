Eid Reunion 2006

 

 

Program Summary

 

 

This program was held on November 11, 2006 at Togoolu College Holmes Auditorium, Jackson, Mississippi. The first part of the program included a children's fashion show, 

followed by a children's cultural program.  

Following children's program, local and guest artists entertained the audience with 

recitation, jokes, solo, duet and chorus songs, and a solo Bangla drama.
