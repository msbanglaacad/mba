Welcome to MBA

Welcome to the official website of Mississippi Bangla Academy (MBA). Founded in 1997, MBA is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the Bengali community in the greater Mississippi area. For more than 25 years, we have worked to preserve and promote Bangla language, arts, and culture while fostering unity, education, and cultural exchange within the broader community.

 

Our Mission and Objectives

The objectives of Mississippi Bangla Academy are to:

·         Offer Bangla language and fine arts education to children of Bengali descent, as well as interested individuals from other communities.

·         Promote Bangla arts and cultural heritage in Mississippi and throughout the United States.

·         Encourage friendship, social interaction, and strong community relationships among Bengali and non-Bengali communities.

·         Support outreach activities that strengthen cultural understanding and community engagement.

Programs and Activities

MBA organizes a variety of educational and cultural programs throughout the year, including:

·         Bangla language classes for children and youth.

·         Cultural workshops and fine arts activities.

·         Celebration of Bengali festivals and national events.

·         Community gatherings and social events.
